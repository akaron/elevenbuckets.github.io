Inspired by existing fully on-chain exchanges such as Kyber Network and Bancor, we introduce an alternative implementation with two new concepts:

  1. discrete dynamic price range that allows near real-time price update without order maker micro-managing the listing price
  2. incentivised on-chain clearhouse via smart contract, which encourages participation of the platform day-to-day operation by sharing profit with participants.

It is our current plan to build 11be on top of Ethereum blockchain.
